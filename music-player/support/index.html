How do I add my music?

Open Wi-Fi Upload in the app, connect your computer to the same Wi-Fi network, then open the shown address in your computer's browser and enter the pairing code. Drag and drop your audio files to transfer them — no cable needed.

Which formats are supported?

MP3, M4A, FLAC, WAV, and AAC.

Does it play in the background?

Yes. Playback continues in the background and on the Lock Screen, with full play / pause / next / previous controls.

Does it need an internet connection?

No. Your music plays fully offline. Wi-Fi Upload uses your local network only — nothing is sent to any server.

如何添加我的音乐？

在 App 中打开 Wi-Fi 上传，让电脑连接到同一个 Wi-Fi 网络，然后在电脑浏览器中打开页面显示的地址并输入配对码，把音频文件拖进去即可传输——无需数据线。

支持哪些格式？

MP3、M4A、FLAC、WAV、AAC。

支持后台播放吗？

支持。App 可在后台和锁屏继续播放，并提供播放 / 暂停 / 上一首 / 下一首控制。

需要联网吗？

不需要。音乐完全离线播放。Wi-Fi 上传仅使用本地网络，不会向任何服务器发送数据。
