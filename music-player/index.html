Music Player — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Data Collection

Music Player does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All music files are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to any server.

Wi-Fi Upload

The Wi-Fi Upload feature runs a local server on your device's local network only. Files transferred via this feature are saved directly to your device and are not sent to any third party.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Contact

音乐播放器 — 隐私政策

最后更新：2026年6月23日

数据收集

音乐播放器不收集、存储或传输任何个人数据。所有音乐文件均存储在您的设备本地，不会上传到任何服务器。

Wi-Fi 上传功能仅在您设备的本地网络中运行。通过此功能传输的文件直接保存到您的设备，不会发送给任何第三方。

第三方服务

本应用不使用任何第三方分析、广告或追踪服务。
