TT Scoreboard — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Data Collection

TT Scoreboard does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app does not require an account or any personally identifiable information.

Local Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Language preference (English or Chinese)
  • Whether the gesture tutorial has been shown

This data never leaves your device and is not shared with anyone.

Network Access

TT Scoreboard does not connect to the internet. No data is sent to any server.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Contact

If you have any questions, please contact: fangvsyu@gmail.com

TT Scoreboard — 隐私政策

最后更新：2026年6月23日

数据收集

TT Scoreboard 不收集、存储或传输任何个人数据。本应用无需账号或任何个人身份信息。

本地存储

本应用仅在您的设备本地存储以下数据：

  • 语言偏好（英文或中文）

这些数据不会离开您的设备，也不会与任何人共享。

网络访问

TT Scoreboard 不连接互联网，不会向任何服务器发送数据。

第三方服务

本应用不使用任何第三方分析、广告或追踪服务。
